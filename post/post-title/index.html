Introduction

Jan 21, 2017 #narrative

At the dawn of the digital age, the Terran homeworld was in a period of intense ideological turmoil. In the first great war of ideas, collectivism was as pervasive as air and water. Nation states persistently moved in the direction of a single global civilization. Might made right and individuals fell into despair.

Those individuals who understood natural law were few and far between. What they lacked in organation, they made up for in raw imagination. Many times tranformation was within grasp, if only they could connect - if only they had a way to see amity works.
